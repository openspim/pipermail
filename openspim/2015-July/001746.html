I started a page on the OpenSPIM wiki:

     http://openspim.org/Publications

However, I feel a wiki should be edited by more than one person, so I 
did not do all that much on that page myself, still waiting for others 
to simply go and add their papers or papers related to OpenSPIM that

To answer your question, I turned to Google Scholar 
(https://scholar.google.com/) and searched for the term "OpenSPIM". 
Among the many reviews, there seem to be a couple of interesting "real" 
biology papers, e.g.:

     
http://biomedicaloptics.spiedigitallibrary.org/article.aspx?articleid=2241619

and a mailing list.

So here is drinking to a vibrant community in the future!
