The 5th annual meeting of the developer's workshop for light-sheet microscopy 
will meet for the first time in the USA at Zeiss headquarters in Thornwood, NY 
September 15-17th, 2013.  This workshop was started by developers of light-
sheet microscopes and will feature talks on instrument development, optical 
design, specimen preparation, 3D reconstruction software and applications for 
light-sheet microscopy.  If you are interested in learning about this new, 
emerging technology you are invited to attend.  Enrollment is limited and there 
is no cost to register.  Details are available at the following URL:  
http://mousecochlea.umn.edu/LSFM2013.php
