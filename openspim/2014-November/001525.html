Dear Frédéric,

The parts page says to use a clean up filter for the 488 diode laser, as
it leaks an unmeasurable amount of green light (which happens to be in
the same range as the emission of GFP) I would recommend a 490/20
excitation filter from either Semrock or Chroma to block this unwanted
light.
