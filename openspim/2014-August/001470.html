Dear EMBO course participants!

We are really excited to welcome you to the EMBO practical course about
light sheet microscopy!

In preparation for the course, we added a couple of pages to the OpenSPIM
wiki; the top-level entry is this page:

	http://openspim.org/EMBO_practical_course_Light_sheet_microscopy

Also, we subscribed you all to the OpenSPIM mailing list; this is the best
way to communicate OpenSPIM related topics, such as the mail you are
reading right now!

We also created accounts and user pages for all of you; please have a
look:

	http://openspim.org/EMBO_2014_Participants

You should have received a mail with an activation link by now; please
activate your account now unless you have already done so.

At this stage, I would like to ask you to find your user page and edit it
(if you have not edited any wiki yet and need help with this, please have
a look at http://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Help:Contents and do not hesitate
to ask questions about it on the OpenSPIM mailing list:
openspim at openspim.org). Feel free to describe your EMBO course project on
your user page, and if you need to upload images to make it look awesome,
go for it!

On behalf of the EMBO course team,
Johannes
