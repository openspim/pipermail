Yeah, 1.4.22 is definitely wrong, most likely a typo (or preparing for 
the next release which is due July 31st).

The version you have as per the Micro-Manager-dev update site would be 
most appropriately described as 1.4.22-SNAPSHOT, i.e. a volatile version 
leading up to 1.4.22.

> I'm asking because I'm having issues with Micromanager, more precisely
> with Tools->Pixel Size Calibration, and I'm wondering whether going to
> an older or newer version would remove them.

The Micro-Manager developers are extremely helpful and nice, and they 
are very responsive (but keep in mind that they are in a different time 
zone: San Francisco, i.e. 9 hours behind us). I would suggest to write a 
mail to micro-manager-general at lists.sourceforge.net that describes the 
problem precisely -- ideally in such a manner that it is easy for any 
reader to reproduce the problem quickly. In my experience, most such bug 
descriptions on the mailing list are addressed swiftly.
