I was wondering if anybody knows a good reference that describes the 
beam profile of the light sheet at the focus of an objective lens. I

cylindrical lens is focused on the back focal plane of the objective lens?

being a hyperbola instead of Dodt's standard Gaussian beam waist 
profile.  Which one is correct for the cylindrical lens focus?

Either way, it would be great to know how to correctly model the 
expected light sheet coming out of the objective.
