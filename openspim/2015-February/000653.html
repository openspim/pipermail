Dear Micro-Manager Users,

As we previously announced, later this year we are going to move the
core development of Micro-Manager from UCSF to a small company that we
will be founding soon, in order to ensure the long-term continuation and
success of Micro-Manager. You can read our previous announcement here:
http://micro-manager.3463995.n2.nabble.com/The-future-of-Micro-Manager-tp7584449.html

We are going to keep Micro-Manager free and open source, and plan to
support its development mainly through paid user support. Your
subscription to the support service will help ensure that Micro-Manager
remains a relevant and useful program and continues to improve.

Please take a moment to preview our preliminary support programs 
(subject to
change) here:
http://bit.ly/1vk83Ff

We want to ensure that these programs are truly useful to you, and would
like to get your feedback on how they are structured. Toward that end, 
we
would very much appreciate your participation in a short survey about 
these
programs and other aspects of the future of Micro-Manager.

Here is the link to the survey:

                        http://bit.ly/17gD1Jf


Thank you very much for spending a moment of your time on this!
Please also feel free to contact us directly with feedback at
info at micro-manager.org.

Please also forward this email to anybody you know who is a
Micro-Manager user but is not subscribed to this mailing list.


Best Regards,
The Micro-Manager Team
Mark Tsuchida, Chris Weisiger, Nico Stuurman
